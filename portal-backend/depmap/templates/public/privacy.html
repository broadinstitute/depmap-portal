Privacy Policy


The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the DEPMAP website depmap.org:

Information gathering

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website by identifying (1) which parts of our site are most heavily used, and (2) which portion of our audience comes from within the Broad Institute network. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means that user sessions will be tracked, but the users will remain anonymous. We use cookies to record information about your online preferences that allow us to personalize your experience of the website. You can control your use of cookies from your web browser, but if you choose not to accept cookies you will not be able to take full advantage of all of the website's features.
